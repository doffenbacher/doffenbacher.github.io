Banana Split Breakfast

  • 1/4 cup Steel Cut Oats
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbs of cacao beans
  • Â½ cup unsweetened coconut yogurt
    http://sodeliciousdairyfree.com/products/cultured-coconut-milk/unsweetened-cultured-coconut-milk
  • honey or maple syrup

Directions

Bring water to a boil. Add oats, reduce heat, and cook 10-20 minutes (depending on how chewy you like your cereal). Stir occasionally. Cover and remove from heat and let stand a few minutes. Makes 1 cup of cereal. Rinse and slice strawberries, slice banana and chop up cacao beans. Arrange fruit around oats and coconut yogurt sprinkle with chopped cacao beans and drizzle with honey.
