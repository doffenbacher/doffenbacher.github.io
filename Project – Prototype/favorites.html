Green Juice

Ingredients

  • Lettuces –
    • Red or Green Leaf Lettuce - 2-3 leaves
    • Romaine (¼ – ½ head)
    • Endive - 2-3 leaves
    • Escarole - 2-3 leaves
    • Beet Tops - 2-3 young inner leaves
    • Watercress - 2-4 leaves
    • Swiss Chard - 2 leaves
  • Green Bell Pepper - ¼ of a medium pepper
  • Granny Smith Apple - 1 medium
  • **Optional - Beet Root (will turn green juice red)**

Preparation:

  1. Rinse off greens of sand or soil
  2. Shake off water and trim ends
  3. Chop leaves into ½” ribbons
  4. Cut up apple to remove seeds and stem
  5. Run lettuce, pepper and apple though juicer

Enjoy juice as soon as finished juicing on an empty stomach to absorb all the live enzymes and nutrients. Enzymes are required for every chemical action that takes place in our body. Our ability to utilize vitamins and minerals is run by enzymes. We need enzymes for proper digestion and cell repair. Our natural enzymes diminish as we age.

What do all plants have in common? Plants are the only type of organism that take light energy from the sun and convert it into sugar. This process is called photosynthesis. Drinking juice is like drinking pure energy.
